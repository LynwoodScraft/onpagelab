## OnPageLab — built by an SEO, for SEOs

I built OnPageLab out of frustration. Existing on-page SEO analysis tools are either too complex or too shallow. I was missing a fast, accurate, free tool that delivers an actionable on-page diagnosis in 30 seconds — no sign-up, no subscription.

My name is **Pierre Aublet**. I'm an SEO Expert based in Paris with 6+ years of experience in organic search. OnPageLab is the tool I wished I'd had from day one.

---

## Who I am

Since **February 2026**, I'm **SEO Expert at [OVHcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com)**, Europe's leading cloud provider, where I drive organic search strategy for one of the most competitive domains in the tech industry.

Before OVHcloud, I spent 5+ years at **[webloom](https://webloom.fr)**, a Paris-based SEO agency, as SEO Team Leader — working with dozens of clients across travel, technology, healthcare, cloud and retail. I'm also an **SEO trainer at Media Institute** (Paris) since May 2025.

**Companies I've worked with:** OVHcloud, Odigo, Samboat, Yousign, Sessile.fr, Gong, France Verif.

---

## What I do

**Technical SEO** — I audit crawlability, indexation, internal linking, Core Web Vitals, migrations, canonical tags and structured data. My daily tools: Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Search Console.

**Content & Semantic Analysis** — I build keyword strategies, analyze search intent and optimize semantic coverage page by page. I design pillar/cluster content architectures and track ranking performance over time.

**Web Analytics** — I audit Google Analytics (Universal & GA4), implement tracking via Google Tag Manager and build Google Data Studio dashboards to measure organic performance.

**Development & Automation** — I work with HTML, CSS, PHP and Python to automate SEO audits, analyze datasets and script repetitive tasks. It's a skill that lets me go further, faster than most SEOs.

## Why OnPageLab?

> "On-page issues are often the fastest to fix and the most impactful in the short term. Yet most free tools don't go deep enough. OnPageLab is the tool I wished I'd had from day one."

OnPageLab is a **personal, independent project** built from real audit experience. Every feature maps to a genuine need I've encountered on client work: measuring title pixel length (not characters), visualizing the heading tree, detecting accidental noindex directives, evaluating keyword density page by page.

It's free because quality SEO resources should be accessible to everyone — freelancers, agencies, students, independents.
