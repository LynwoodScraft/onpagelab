## What OnPageLab analyzes

### Technical SEO audit
Instant detection of critical issues: missing or oversized title tag, absent meta description, duplicate H1, HTTP status codes, redirect chains.

### Semantic analysis
Calculates keyword density in content, headings, title tag and alt attributes. Identifies LSI terms and semantic coverage gaps.

### Heading structure (H1–H6)
Validates your heading hierarchy: H1 uniqueness, depth, keyword presence, logical flow from H1 to H3.

### Internal linking
Counts internal and external links, detects broken links, analyzes anchor text quality. Linking score with recommendations.

### Meta tags optimization
Pixel-accurate length measurement for title and meta description. Open Graph and Twitter Card detection. Live SERP preview.

### Global SEO score
A weighted score from 0 to 100 aggregating all five axes. Identify at a glance what's holding your page back.

## FAQ

**What is on-page SEO?**
On-page SEO refers to all optimization factors directly present on a web page: the title tag, meta description, heading structure, keyword density, content, internal links and technical attributes. Unlike off-page SEO (link building), on-page SEO is entirely under your control.

**Is OnPageLab really free?**
Yes. The core on-page analysis is 100% free with no account required. Enter a URL, get your report instantly. A Pro plan with history, PDF exports and bulk analysis is coming soon.

**How long does an analysis take?**
About 30 seconds for a standard page. OnPageLab crawls the page in real time, parses the DOM, analyzes the content and generates the report — all server-side for reliable results.

**Can OnPageLab analyze any website?**
Yes, as long as the page is publicly accessible. You can analyze your own pages, competitors' pages, or any public URL.
